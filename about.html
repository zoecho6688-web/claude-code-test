Shanghai Midu Measurement and Control Technology Co., Ltd. (MEDO) is a comprehensive high-precision positioning and IoT technology automated monitoring service provider under Shenzhen BDStar Navigation. We specialize in Beidou high-precision positioning and deformation monitoring.

We serve four primary industries: Water Conservancy, National Land Geohazards, Mining Safety, and Urban Infrastructure. We provide a "Five-in-One" integrated solution encompassing Hardware + Software + IoT + Implementation & Operation + Integration Consulting. We are dedicated to promoting the deep application of Beidou-based IoT monitoring and digital twin simulation technology, creating multi-dimensional value for customers and driving industry-wide digitalization and intelligent development through data and business dual-engines.
