01 / Industry Pain Points

Key Pain Points in Tailing Dam Safety

Tailings storage facilities represent one of the highest-risk structures in the mining industry. Traditional inspection methods are too slow, too infrequent, and too imprecise for modern safety demands.

Invisible Seepage Buildup

Pore water pressure accumulates silently inside dam walls. Manual piezometer readings are too infrequent to catch rapid saturation events before they become critical.

Slow Deformation Detection

Surface displacement of even a few millimeters can signal imminent slope failure. Periodic survey methods miss the early warning window entirely.

Delayed Emergency Response

Without automated alerting, operators rely on manual checks. By the time an anomaly is reported, the response window may already be closed.

Fragmented Data Silos

Rainfall, tilt, GNSS, and seepage data are collected by separate systems with no unified view, making holistic risk assessment nearly impossible.
