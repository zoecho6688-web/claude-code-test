01 / Project Overview

Project Challenge

Situated in a prime waterfront extension zone along the Huangpu River, in the heart of Shanghai, this project is as prestigious as it is complex. The primary challenge lay in the extreme density of the surrounding urban environment and the volatile geological conditions of the riverfront. Nestling a deep foundation pit for a future "Tech Innovation Highland" within such a sensitive area meant that even the slightest subterranean shift could have cascading effects on critical city infrastructure. Traditional, manual monitoring was no longer an option; the project demanded a solution capable of navigating immense structural pressure and high-frequency data requirements without a single point of failure.

Faced with these intricate risks, Medo stepped forward with unwavering resolve to push the boundaries of structural health monitoring technology. Our mission was definitive: to transform a high-risk construction site into a transparent, digitally-safeguarded ecosystem. We did not merely deploy sensors; we engineered a sophisticated "digital central nervous system" for the entire construction site. From precision hardware deployment to proprietary software development, Medo delivered a comprehensive, full-lifecycle foundation pit monitoring management solution. In the most demanding urban environments, we proved that innovation and safety can harmoniously coexist through the power of intelligent technology.

02 / Full-Stack Solution

Monitoring Items & Solution

Retaining Wall Displacement

Automated tracking of horizontal and vertical shifts of wall coping to ensure crest stability.

Column Vertical Settlement

Monitoring differential settlement of internal support columns to prevent uneven structural stress.

Deep-seated Lateral Deformation

Inclinometer-based sensing of soil movement at varying depths behind the retaining structure.

Groundwater Level Monitoring

Piezometric sensing of water table fluctuations outside the pit to assess seepage risks.

Concrete Support Axial Force

Vibrating wire strain gauges measure internal stress within concrete struts to detect overloading.

Integrated Lifecycle Health Strategy

Medo’s solution for large-scale structural health monitoring focuses on the correlation between multi-source sensors. By synchronizing underground soil mechanics with surface stress data, we provide a unified intelligence layer that predicts structural failure long before it becomes visible.

03 / Outcomes

Outcomes & Impact

Beyond the immediate safety outcomes, the project demonstrated that high-precision GNSS monitoring infrastructure can be successfully deployed and sustained in sub-Saharan African mining environments with minimal on-site technical expertise requirements.

The success of this deployment has since informed the design standard for Medo's subsequent African mining projects, particularly with respect to communications redundancy architecture and multi-language alert routing.
