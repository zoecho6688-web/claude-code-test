01 / Industry Pain Points

The Hidden Risks in Tunneling

Tunnel structures are subject to immense overburden pressure. Traditional manual convergence measurements are inefficient and pose significant safety risks to personnel.

Convergence Deformation

Subtle inward movement of tunnel walls signals structural yielding. Manual inspections often miss these rapid trends.

Personnel Safety Hazards

Manual measurements in active traffic or construction zones are hazardous and labor-intensive.
