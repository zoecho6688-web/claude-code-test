01 / Critical Risks

Why Foundation Pits Fail

Deep excavations in urban areas are high-stakes operations. A single structural anomaly can lead to pit wall collapse or catastrophic settlement of neighboring high-rises.

Support Stress Spikes

Internal struts and anchors face unpredictable load changes. Manual strain checks are too slow to catch sudden axial force surges.

Neighboring Settlement

Excavation-induced groundwater shifts cause surrounding soil to consolidate, potentially tilting nearby subway lines or buildings.
