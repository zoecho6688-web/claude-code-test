01 / Industry Pain Points

Why Small Reservoirs Are High-Risk

Small reservoirs and mountain ponds are among the most vulnerable water infrastructure. Their remote locations and limited oversight create conditions where rapid water level changes go undetected until it's too late.

High Frequency of Overtopping (漫坝)

Small reservoirs have limited storage capacity and low freeboard margins. During intense rainfall, water levels can rise faster than manual response allows, leading to overtopping and downstream flooding.

Remote Locations with Poor Connectivity

Mountain ponds and rural reservoirs are often hours from the nearest town. Wired infrastructure is impractical, and manual inspection schedules cannot match the speed of weather-driven events.

Rapid Water Level Surges During Storms

Flash storm events can raise water levels by meters within minutes. Without automated real-time sensing, downstream communities receive no warning before a surge reaches them.

02 / Solution Overview

Integrated Wireless Monitoring

Radar water level sensing, rain gauges, and video AI — unified on a low-power 4G/LoRa transmission platform. Designed for rapid deployment in remote, off-grid locations.

Layer 01

Radar Water Level

Non-contact radar water level gauges provide continuous, maintenance-free measurement. No moving parts, no fouling — reliable through flood debris and algae growth.

Layer 02

Rain Gauge

Tipping bucket rain gauges correlate upstream rainfall with reservoir inflow rates. Early rainfall data enables predictive pre-warning before water levels begin to rise.

Layer 03

Video AI

AI-powered cameras monitor dam crest and spillway conditions visually. Automatic detection of overtopping, debris blockage, and unauthorized access — transmitted via 4G/LoRa.

03 / Core Advantages

Built for Remote Water Infrastructure

Small reservoirs need monitoring that works without power grids, fiber cables, or on-site staff. Midu's solution is engineered for rapid deployment and unattended long-term operation.

01

Rapid Deployment (under 2 hours)

Compact, pre-configured sensor units mount on existing dam structures with no civil works required. A single technician can commission a complete monitoring station in under two hours.

02

Ultra-low Power Consumption (Solar Powered)

All units run on integrated solar panels with battery backup. Designed for years of unattended operation in off-grid mountain locations with no external power supply needed.

03

SMS/App Mobile Alerts for Local Rangers

When water levels approach warning thresholds, automated SMS and app push notifications reach local reservoir rangers and village officials instantly — no internet or desktop required.

04

4G/LoRa Dual Transmission

Where 4G coverage is available, data uploads in real time. In dead zones, LoRa mesh networking relays data to the nearest gateway — ensuring no readings are lost regardless of terrain.
