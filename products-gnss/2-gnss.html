Product Introduction

The Medo M20 is a professional-grade GNSS monitoring receiver designed specifically for long-term, unattended deformation monitoring applications. Unlike survey-grade receivers optimized for short observation sessions, the M20 is built around continuous operation: a ruggedized aluminum housing, wide-temperature electronics, and an integrated power management system that supports solar charging and battery backup for indefinite autonomous deployment.

Supporting all major satellite constellations — GPS, BeiDou, GLONASS, and Galileo — the M20 tracks up to 800 channels simultaneously, delivering robust signal acquisition even in partially obstructed environments such as open-pit mine walls, dam faces, and valley-side slope installations. Phase-differential processing against a fixed reference station achieves horizontal accuracy of ±1mm and vertical accuracy of ±2mm under normal atmospheric conditions.

02 / Features

Key Functions

Real-time Displacement Monitoring

Continuously computes 3D displacement vectors relative to a fixed reference station. Onboard buffering ensures no data loss during communication outages up to 72 hours.

Multi-constellation Signal Tracking

Simultaneously tracks GPS, BeiDou, GLONASS, and Galileo signals across 800 channels. Multi-constellation redundancy maintains continuity when individual systems degrade.

Automated Threshold Alerting

Onboard alert logic evaluates displacement magnitude, velocity, and acceleration against three-tier thresholds, triggering immediate transmission independent of regular reporting cycles.

Autonomous Power Management

Integrated MPPT solar charge controller with 20Ah LiFePO₄ battery provides up to 5 days of operation without solar input. Power status reported alongside displacement data.

Dual-channel Communications

Primary 4G LTE with automatic satellite fallback ensures data continuity in areas with unreliable cellular coverage. Local LoRa radio enables peer-to-peer mesh networking between units.

Remote Configuration & OTA Update

All parameters configurable remotely via the Medo cloud platform. Firmware updates delivered over-the-air with rollback capability, minimizing maintenance visits to remote sites.
