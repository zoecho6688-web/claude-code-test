01 / Critical Challenges

Brittle Failure Dynamics

Sudden Brittle Failure

Unlike gradual landslides, rock masses fail instantaneously. Our sensing focuses on the sub-millimeter precursors that define the 'Golden Warning Time'.

High-Risk Accessibility

Vertical rock faces make cabling impossible. Our wireless LoRaWAN terminals enable deployment in inaccessible terrains.

Meteorological Triggers

Freeze-thaw cycles and intense rain act as final catalysts. Real-time correlation is vital for false-alarm mitigation.
